Instructions

Welcome to the Hangman Game!

Objective: Guess the hidden word by selecting letters. You have a limited number of wrong guesses before the game is over.

How to Play:
- Click on letters to guess them.
- Correct guesses reveal the letters in the word.
- Incorrect guesses add parts to the hangman drawing.
- The game ends when you guess the word or the hangman drawing is complete.

Good luck and have fun!
